Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 19, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Kevin D. Franklin Email Assistant ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our email management service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Name and email address when you register
  • Password (encrypted and never stored in plain text)
  • Account preferences and settings

2.2 Email Account Access

  • Email provider credentials (encrypted)
  • OAuth tokens for Gmail, Outlook, and other providers (encrypted)
  • Email metadata (sender, recipient, subject, date)
  • Email content for analysis and draft generation

2.3 Usage Information

  • Log data including IP address, browser type, and access times
  • Service usage patterns and features accessed
  • AI processing requests and responses

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our email management service
  • Analyze emails for priority, urgency, and relevance
  • Generate draft responses using AI
  • Send notifications about urgent or important emails
  • Improve our service and develop new features
  • Communicate with you about your account and service updates
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All email credentials and OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • HTTPS: All data transmission is encrypted using TLS/SSL
  • Access Control: Strict authentication and authorization controls
  • Database Security: Hosted on secure cloud infrastructure (Neon PostgreSQL)
  • Regular Audits: Ongoing security assessments and updates

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Email Providers

We connect to your email accounts through official APIs and protocols (OAuth 2.0, IMAP/SMTP). We comply with each provider's terms of service and security requirements.

5.2 AI Processing

We use Anthropic's Claude AI to analyze emails and generate draft responses. Email content is processed according to Anthropic's privacy policy and is not used to train AI models.

5.3 Hosting and Infrastructure

  • Vercel - Application hosting
  • Neon - Database hosting

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Email Data: Retained for service functionality; you can delete at any time
  • Logs: Retained for 90 days for security and debugging purposes

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data
  • Disconnect email accounts at any time
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • With service providers who are bound by confidentiality agreements

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies for:

  • Authentication (JWT tokens)
  • Session management
  • User preferences

We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily stored in the United States. By using our service, you consent to the transfer of your information to the US and other countries where our service providers operate.

11. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or through our service. Your continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act, including:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

14. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

For users in the European Union, we comply with GDPR requirements:

  • Lawful basis for processing: Consent and contract performance
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to be forgotten
  • Right to object to processing

15. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@kevindfranklin.com
  • Website: www.kevindfranklin.com/contact
